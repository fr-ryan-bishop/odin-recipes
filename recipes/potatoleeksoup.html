Potato and Leek Soup

Description

A wonderful addition to any autumn menu, this recipe is sure to please, and on a tight budget too!

Ingredients:

  • 3 large leeks
  • 1/2 package of bacon, chopped
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 2 lbs potatos
  • 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • pinch of marjoram
  • 1-2 sprigs of freshthyme (or 1/2 tsp dried)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tbsp dried)
  • splash of tobasco
  • pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Fry bacon and set aside, reserving 2 tbsp of bacon grease.
  2. Cook leeks in reserved bacon grease ~5-7 min.
  3. Add remaining indgredients and simmer 20 minutes.
  4. Blend with hand blender
  5. Serve with sour cream, green onion, hot sauce, and croutons.
