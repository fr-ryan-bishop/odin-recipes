Karen's Soft-Shelled Texas Tacos

Description

This recipe is a true crowd pleaser! Adults and children alike will enjoy the delicious tangy Tex-Mex rendering of an old classic.

Ingredients

Sauce:
  • 20oz tomato juice
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 2 lbs chicken
Shells
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps:

Beef
  1. In a skillet, brown beef.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients except brown sugar, then add to browned beef.
  3. Stir, then add sugar once combined.
  4. Simmer.
Shells
  1. Mix eggs, milk, butter, and salt.
  2. Add cornmeal and mix.
  3. Add flour and mix.
  4. Fry 1/4 cup batter per shell in a well-greased skillet.
