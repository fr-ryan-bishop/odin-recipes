Classic Beef Stew

Description

This hearty stew is perfect for warming you up on a cold winter day; its sure to stick to your ribs!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs round steak
  • 4 medium potatos
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 3 tbsp minit tapioca
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 28 oz can of crushed tomatos
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Rough chop all vegetables.
  2. Add tomatos, tapioca, sugar, and beef broth.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook in a crockpot for 8 hours on low.
  5. Enjoy!
